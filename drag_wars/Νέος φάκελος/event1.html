The Event

For the first time in Greece, brought to you by The Drag-Wars Group, this event gives car enthusiasts a unique opportunity to race their cars head-to-head and against the most strict judge..the clock! You will also have the chance to meet each other so you can share your experience, your knowledge and your passion for the cars.

In this greek celebration of drag racing will also participate amongst the biggest greek tuners, some of the most well-known tuners in the world.
